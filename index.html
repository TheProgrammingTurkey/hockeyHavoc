Instructions

User Team is the Red Team

User Team Shoots to the Right

Blue Outline is User

Red Outline is the User's Teammates

Green Outline is the CPU

Face Offs

When the face off circle turns green, press the space bar before your opponent to win the draw

Shooting

When the space bar is pressed, the puck will be shot/passed the direction the user is facing
